Professionalism Policy

Notice
This policy was written and reviewed by the students enrolled in Website Development at Bartlett High School. The Policies held within this
document can be modified or removed by any Bartlett High School Staff. Staff members also have the right to interpret and enforce these policies.

Bill Of Professionalism

A. Students will be productive and work oriented.
B. Students will take care of their equipment and will not abuse technology.
C. Students will be respectful to each other and web design clients.
D. Students will not have food or drink in the classroom.
E. Students will maintain a clean workspace and keep cords neat.
F. Students will bring their laptop everyday to class.
G. Students will turn their in projects on time.
H. Students will not distract each other.
I. Students will be on time to class and maintain good attendance to school.
J. Students will be responsible for work missed on days they are absent.
